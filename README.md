🛒 Amazon Product Sales Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Amazon product sales, customer reviews, and category performance. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor revenue trends, product demand, customer engagement, and marketplace performance through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

🎯 Business Objectives
Monitor sales performance across product categories.
Analyze monthly and weekly sales trends.
Track product demand and customer reviews.
Identify top-performing products.
Compare category-wise revenue contribution.
Support data-driven product and sales decisions.
